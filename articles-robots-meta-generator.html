Best Practices for Robots Meta Tags

To optimize your robots meta tags, follow these tips:

  • Use Noindex Wisely: Apply to duplicate or private pages.
  • Avoid Overuse: Don’t block all crawling.
  • Test Impact: Check with crawler simulators.
  • Combine with Robots.txt: Use both tools strategically.
  • Update Regularly: Adjust for site changes.
