❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is donating mandatory to use VIN NESIA tools?

No, absolutely not! All our tools are completely free to use. Donations are entirely voluntary and help us maintain and improve our services.

Where do the donated funds go?

Your donations help us cover hosting costs, domain renewals, server maintenance, and development of new features. We're committed to keeping our tools free for everyone.

How secure are these donation methods?

All recommended platforms (Wise, Remitly, WorldRemit, etc.) use bank-level security with HTTPS encryption. We never store your payment information.

Is there a minimum donation amount?

There's no minimum amount! Every contribution, no matter how small, helps us keep the lights on. Even 1,000 IDR makes a difference.

How will I know my donation was received?

Send your donation confirmation to support@vinnesia.my.id or admin@vinnesia.my.id. We'll confirm receipt within 24 hours.

Can I get a receipt for tax purposes?

As we're not a registered charity organization, we can't provide tax-deductible receipts. However, we can provide a simple donation confirmation for your records.

What currencies do you accept?

We primarily accept Indonesian Rupiah (IDR) and US Dollars (USD). Other currencies can be converted through international transfer services like Wise or Remitly.

Can I donate from outside Indonesia?

Yes! We accept international donations through Wise, Remitly, WorldRemit, Western Union, and other international transfer services to our SeaBank account.
