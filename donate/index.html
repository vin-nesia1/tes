Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about donating to VIN NESIA.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Currently, VIN NESIA does not have tax-exempt status, so donations are not tax-deductible. We are working towards this goal in the future.

How will my donation be used?

Your donation will support server costs, development of new tools, and maintenance of existing services to keep them free for everyone.

What if I face issues with the donation process?

Please contact us via WhatsApp at +62895354511777 or email at support@vinnesia.my.id. We'll assist you promptly.

Is there a minimum donation amount?

No, there is no minimum donation amount. Every contribution, no matter how small, is greatly appreciated and helps us maintain our free services.

Can I donate from outside Indonesia?

Yes! We accept international donations through Wise, Remitly, WorldRemit, Western Union, and other international transfer services to our SeaBank account.

Will I receive a receipt for my donation?

Yes, we will send you a confirmation receipt via WhatsApp or email once we receive your donation. This helps us keep track of our supporters.
