Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 29, 2025, 10:56 PM WIB

Introduction

At VIN NESIA, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website at vinnesia.my.id. Our website provides free online tools to enhance your productivity, and we do not require user registration, login, or personal data submission through forms. We utilize services like Google AdSense and Google Analytics, and you can learn more about their practices at policies.google.com.

This policy complies with Indonesia’s Personal Data Protection Law (UU No. 27 Tahun 2022) and aligns with Google AdSense requirements regarding cookies, third-party tracking, and advertisements.

Information We Collect

VIN NESIA does not collect or store personal data directly from users, as our website does not feature login systems, registration forms, or mechanisms to collect personal information such as names, addresses, or payment details. However, we use third-party services that may collect non-personal data as described below:

  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies to enhance your experience, such as remembering your preferences (e.g., cookie consent). Third-party services like Google may also collect IP addresses and user activity data (e.g., pages visited) for analytics and advertising purposes.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous data about website usage, such as page views, time spent on the site, and general location (e.g., country or region). This may include IP addresses, processed anonymously.
  • Google AdSense: Our website displays advertisements through Google AdSense, which may use cookies, IP addresses, and user activity data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your browsing history and interests.

Cookies and Third-Party Tracking

Cookies are used to improve your browsing experience and provide relevant advertisements. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. Third-party services like Google AdSense use cookies to deliver personalized ads based on your interests.

Types of Cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly (e.g., remembering your cookie consent choice).
  • Analytics Cookies: Used by Google Analytics to collect anonymized data for performance analysis.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense and its partners to deliver relevant ads based on your interests.

You can manage your cookie preferences through the cookie consent banner displayed on your first visit or by adjusting your browser settings. To opt out of personalized ads, visit Google’s Ads Settings or use the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

How We Use Your Information

The data collected through cookies and Google Analytics is used to:

  • Analyze website performance and user behavior to improve our tools and content.
  • Provide a better user experience by remembering your preferences.
  • Deliver relevant advertisements through Google AdSense.

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties for purposes other than those outlined in this policy.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, which may collect data as described in their privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics. See Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements. See Google’s Advertising Policies and Ad Technology Policy.

These third parties may use cookies, web beacons, or other technologies to collect data for analytics and advertising purposes. We encourage you to review their privacy policies for more information.

Data Protection and Security

As we do not collect personal data directly, our focus is on ensuring that third-party services we use comply with applicable data protection laws, including Indonesia’s UU No. 27 Tahun 2022 on Personal Data Protection. We rely on reputable providers like Google, which implement industry-standard security measures.

If you have concerns about data collected by third parties, you can contact us at privacy@vinnesia.my.id or reach out directly to the third-party providers.

Your Rights

Under Indonesia’s Personal Data Protection Law, you have rights regarding your personal data. Since VIN NESIA does not collect or store personal data directly, you may exercise your rights (e.g., access, correction, or deletion) with respect to data collected by third-party services like Google Analytics or Google AdSense. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for instructions on how to exercise these rights.

You can also manage your data preferences by:

  • Adjusting your browser’s cookie settings.
  • Opting out of personalized ads via Google’s Ads Settings.
  • Using tools like the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
