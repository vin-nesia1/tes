Best Practices for Image Editing

To achieve the best results, follow these tips:

  • Maintain Resolution: Avoid over-compressing to preserve quality.
  • Use Layers: Edit non-destructively for flexibility.
  • Balance Colors: Adjust brightness and contrast naturally.
  • Test Formats: Choose the right format (JPEG for photos, PNG for transparency).
  • Backup Files: Keep originals before applying major changes.
