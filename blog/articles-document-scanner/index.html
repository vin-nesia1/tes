Best Practices for Scanning Documents

To get the best results, follow these tips:

  • Good Lighting: Use bright, even lighting to avoid shadows.
  • Clean Surface: Ensure the document is flat and free of creases.
  • High Resolution: Select a higher DPI for detailed documents.
  • Regular Updates: Back up digital files periodically.
  • Secure Storage: Use encrypted cloud services for sensitive data.
